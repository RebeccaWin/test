So, who am I?

I'm a 25-year-old passionate dancer and curious traveler who has been dancing since the age of 6. With a lifelong passion for dance, I've honed my skills in various dance forms over the years, including jazz, hip-hop, and contemporary dance.

Aside from dance, I also have a deep love for exploring the world and learning about different cultures. I enjoy immersing myself in the local culture and trying new foods and discovering hidden gems that are off the beaten path.

With a curious mind and a thirst for knowledge, I always seek new experiences and opportunities to learn. Whether it's trying a new dance style or learning a new language, I'm always eager to expand my horizons.

Having worked in the service industry for several years, I've developed a strong passion for helping people and meeting new faces. This is a trait that I have carried forward into my career as a UX/UI designer.

One of the reasons I was drawn to UX-design was my love for creativity and my desire to help people. The idea of designing user experiences that are not only visually appealing but also solve real-world problems and make life easier for people really excites me!
